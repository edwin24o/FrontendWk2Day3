Black Myth Wukong

What is Black Myth Wukong??

The highly anticipated action RPG inspired by the legendary Chinese novel Journey to the West which has inspired hundreds of characters throughtout the years. Developed by Game Science Studio, which delivers (and DELIVERS) an immersive experience with stunning visuals, fast-paced gameplay, and awesome character designs. The game has captured the attention of fans of inspired characters, single game players and lover of anctient lore worldwide, promising a fresh take on the classic story. The game is sure to be the runner up for game of the year. It has the action gameplay in the mist of some Soul like games and the new God of war fracnhise. The graphics are far from the average, making waves and setting the new bar for games to come. Be sure to check this game out if you are ready for an immense and profound experience with tough and agonizing bosses that will leave you pattern recognizing for hours or maybe days.

Pros and Cons of Black Myth: Wukong

Pros

  • Action gameplay
  • Stunning graphics
  • Amazing story
  • Great build your own class

Cons

  • Slight learning curve at first
  • Occasional bugs (new games tend to have but nothing to drastic)
  • High system requirements
